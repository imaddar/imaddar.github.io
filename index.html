Field notes

Hey, I’m Imad—always tinkering, learning, and sharing the wins (and bloopers). Most days I bounce between CU Boulder classes, co-founding DAGR Tech, and experimenting with side quests like tiny ML runtimes or RF signal toys. This corner of the internet is my running log of projects, reflections, and open invitations to collaborate.

Current adventures include co-building privacy-first routers for deployed service members, digging into physics-informed ML on campus, and helping non-profits automate the unglamorous chores through Blueprint Boulder. When I need a break, I mentor HackCU teams, scribble down questions that keep me up, and spin up odd experiments in reinforcement learning or observability just to see what happens.
